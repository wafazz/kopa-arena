# Kopa Arena — System Features Summary

## Booking Management
- **Online Booking** — Customers book facilities directly from the website
- **Walk-in Booking** — Staff can create bookings on the spot (POS-style)
- **Match Booking** — Split cost between two teams; one team books, opponent joins later
- **Double Booking Prevention** — System automatically blocks overlapping bookings
- **Booking Approval** — Staff can approve, reject, or cancel bookings
- **Deposit & Full Payment Tracking** — Track payment status per booking
- **Calendar View** — Visual calendar to see all bookings at a glance (daily/weekly/monthly)

## Multi-Branch Support
- **Branch Management** — Add unlimited branches, each with their own facilities
- **Branch Staff Portal** — Branch staff only see their own branch data
- **Role-Based Access** — Superadmin, HQ Staff, Branch Manager, Branch Staff — each with different permissions

## Pricing & Sales
- **Flexible Pricing Rules** — Set different prices by day of week, peak/off-peak hours
- **Per-Branch Pricing** — Assign pricing rules to specific branches
- **Daily Sales Closing** — Close daily sales with full summary and breakdown
- **Day Lock** — Once sales are closed, no more changes allowed for that day

## Check-In System
- **QR Code Check-In** — Each booking gets a unique QR code
- **Staff Scanner** — Staff scan QR codes using phone camera
- **Self Check-In** — Customers can check in themselves 30 minutes before their slot
- **Match Auto Check-In** — Checking in one team automatically checks in the opponent

## Notifications
- **WhatsApp Booking Confirmation** — Instant notification when customer books
- **Booking Reminders** — Automatic WhatsApp reminders at 1 hour, 30 min, and 15 min before the game

## Online Payment
- **SenangPay Integration** — Customers pay online during booking (supports sandbox & production mode)
- **Auto Payment Verification** — System auto-updates payment status after successful payment

## E-Commerce (Shop)
- **Product Catalog** — Sell merchandise, equipment, etc. per branch
- **Product Variations** — Support for size, color, etc. with separate stock tracking
- **Shopping Cart** — Add to cart, update quantity, checkout
- **Order Management** — Track orders from pending to completed
- **Delivery Options** — Pickup from branch or shipping with address

## Reports
- **Booking Reports** — Filter by date range, branch, facility
- **E-Commerce Reports** — Sales and order reports
- **Combined Reports** — See total revenue from both bookings and shop
- **Dashboard** — Real-time stats — today's sales, monthly sales, yearly sales, charts

## Public Website
- **Landing Page** — Professional booking page for customers
- **Branch & Facility Selection** — Choose branch, pick facility, select time slot
- **Real-Time Availability** — See which slots are available, booked, or open for match
- **Live Price Calculator** — Price shown instantly based on selected slot

## Settings & Admin
- **Custom Logo** — Upload your own logo for admin panel and website
- **Staff Permissions** — Control exactly what each staff member can do
- **Facility Management** — Add/edit facilities with custom time slot rules
